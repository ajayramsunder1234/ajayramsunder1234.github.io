Narrative Visualization - Worldwide years of schooling

by Ajay Ramsunder

This narrative visualization shows the average years of schooling in all the different regions in the World. The story that is being shown here states that the average years of schooling has increased through time but also indicates development level in different countries and regions. The data source was taken from https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/mean-years-of-schooling-long-run.
